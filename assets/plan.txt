Comprehensive Muscle-Building & Athletic Performance Plan
Week Structure:

    Day 1: Upper Body Strength + Core Stability
    Day 2: Lower Body Strength + Mobility
    Day 3: Plyometrics + Agility + Core
    Day 4: Rest or Active Recovery
    Day 5: Upper Body Hypertrophy + Rotator Cuff + Neck
    Day 6: Lower Body Hypertrophy + Adductors/Abductors
    Day 7: Functional Training + Cardiovascular Endurance
    Day 8: Rest or Active Recovery
    Repeat Cycle

Day 1: Upper Body Strength + Core Stability

    Chest: Barbell Bench Press – 4 sets x 6 reps
    Back: Weighted Pull-Ups – 4 sets x 6 reps
    Shoulders: Overhead Press – 4 sets x 6 reps
    Upper Traps: Barbell Shrugs – 3 sets x 10 reps
    Core Stability: Plank Variations (front, side) – 3 sets x 45 seconds
    Forearms: Farmer’s Carries – 3 sets x 60 seconds
    Rotator Cuff Activation:
        External Rotations with Band – 3 sets x 15 reps
        Internal Rotations with Band – 3 sets x 15 reps

Day 2: Lower Body Strength + Mobility

    Quads: Barbell Squats – 4 sets x 6 reps
    Hamstrings: Romanian Deadlifts – 4 sets x 8 reps
    Glutes: Hip Thrusts – 4 sets x 8 reps
    Calves: Standing Calf Raises – 4 sets x 15 reps
    Adductors/Abductors: Lateral Band Walks – 3 sets x 15 steps each side
    Mobility Work: Dynamic Stretching and Hip Openers – 15 minutes
    Tibialis Anterior: Toe Raises – 3 sets x 20 reps

Day 3: Plyometrics + Agility + Core

    Plyometrics: Box Jumps – 4 sets x 8 reps
    Agility Drills: Ladder Drills and Cone Drills – 15 minutes
    Functional Movement: Medicine Ball Slams – 3 sets x 12 reps
    Core/Anti-Rotation: Pallof Press – 3 sets x 12 reps each side
    Core Stabilizers: Dead Bugs – 3 sets x 15 reps
    Flexibility: Static Stretching for Lower Body – 10 minutes

Day 4: Rest or Active Recovery

    Activities: Light cardio (walking, cycling), yoga, or mobility exercises
    Flexibility: Full-body stretching routine – 20 minutes

Day 5: Upper Body Hypertrophy + Rotator Cuff + Neck

    Chest: Incline Dumbbell Press – 4 sets x 10 reps
    Back: Cable Rows – 4 sets x 10 reps
    Shoulders:
        Dumbbell Lateral Raises – 3 sets x 12 reps
        Reverse Flyes (Posterior Deltoids) – 3 sets x 12 reps
    Biceps: Hammer Curls – 3 sets x 12 reps
    Triceps: Skull Crushers – 3 sets x 12 reps
    Rotator Cuff:
        External Rotations with Band – 3 sets x 15 reps
        Internal Rotations with Band – 3 sets x 15 reps
    Neck: Neck Flexion/Extension (light weights) – 3 sets x 15 reps
    Lower Traps/Scapular Stabilizers: Prone Y Raises – 3 sets x 12 reps

Day 6: Lower Body Hypertrophy + Adductors/Abductors

    Quads: Leg Press – 4 sets x 10 reps
    Hamstrings: Leg Curls – 4 sets x 10 reps
    Glutes: Bulgarian Split Squats – 3 sets x 10 reps each leg
    Calves: Seated Calf Raises – 4 sets x 15 reps
    Adductors: Cable Adductions – 3 sets x 12 reps each side
    Balance/Stability: Single-Leg Romanian Deadlifts – 3 sets x 10 reps each leg
    Mobility Work: Dynamic Stretches for Lower Body – 10 minutes

Day 7: Functional Training + Cardiovascular Endurance

    Power Movements: Kettlebell Swings – 4 sets x 12 reps
    Functional Movements:
        Sled Pushes/Pulls – 4 sets x 30 meters
        Battle Ropes – 3 sets x 60 seconds
    Core/Obliques: Russian Twists – 3 sets x 15 reps each side
    Forearms: Wrist Curls and Reverse Curls – 3 sets x 15 reps each
    Cardiovascular Endurance: Moderate-intensity cardio (running, cycling, rowing) – 30 minutes
    Flexibility: Full-body static stretching – 15 minutes

Day 8: Rest or Active Recovery

    Activities: Light yoga, stretching, or leisurely walking
    Recovery Techniques: Foam rolling or massage – 20 minutes
